Utopia Main Title Sequence

Direction | Design | Production

2020

Title Sequence

This project began a short time after watching the brilliant 2013 Channel 4’s action/drama series, titled Utopia. You can tell it’s a bit experimental regarding the screenplay and the production. Ironically there are no main title sequences so, I’ve decided to homage the series by making one on my own.

It took roughly 16 weeks to finish, from concept development to delivery.
