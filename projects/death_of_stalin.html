Death of Stalin Main Title Styleframes

Direction | Design | Production

2019

Styleframes

Following the For All Mankind titles, I wanted to create something in the 3D realm, I’ve felt the Death of Stalin (2017) comedy movie, by Armando Iannucci as a great starting point to explore the technique. Being a conceptually and simple project, what I aimed for was a way to visualize the slow decline of power and consequent replacement of power by others who ambitiously seek it in a brutalist regime.
