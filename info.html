Bio

I am Diogo Paixão, motion graphics and CGI generalist.


Currently I’m available for hire as a freelancer in order to develop personal and commercial projects in the realm of the visual media. This ranges from photography to 3D based animation. I'm also based in Porto, Portugal.


By employing a multidisciplinary approach and with a production based workflow in mind, the projects that I’m involved in are usually defined by a strong, filmic and narrative driven momentum. I am a bit more comfortable with production and post-production jobs, when weighting these phases against pre-production or direction tasks. Said jobs might include, Design, Rendering, Modeling, Simulation, Compositing, Color Correction, Video Editing, Animation, Look Development, Concept Development and Pipeline Optimization.


Most levels of my theoretical and technical expertise come from a self-taught environment and a strong will to keep expanding my set of skills.
